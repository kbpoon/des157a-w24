Rules

game rules:

  • there are two players.
  • a player will roll the dice.
  • the total of the roll is added to the current player's score. if a skull is rolled this player's turn is over, and it will switch to the other player.
  • if a player rolls two skulls, their current score gets zeroed out and will have to start from zero.
  • after each successful roll, the current player can either roll again or pass the turn to the other player.
  • the first player to get 30 points or higher wins !!
